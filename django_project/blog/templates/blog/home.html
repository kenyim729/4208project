Introduction




A virtual YouTuber (VTuber), is an online entertainer that uses a (usually anime-inspired) computer graphics-generated avatar. The first entertainer to coin the phrase "virtual YouTuber", Kizuna AI, began live streaming in late 2016, and her popularity started a VTuber trend within Japan and later worldwide. By 2020, there were over 10,000 VTubers.
